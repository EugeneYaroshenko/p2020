Overview

The problem of being productive among many digital distractions around us is something many productivity trackers try to tackle today. But Y-Productive is different. Being a blend of productivity tracker, time tracker, and task management software, it concentrates on what’s really important for a person's workday: actions invested to complete his goal.

Productivity chart is the heart of Y-Productive. It allows user to instantly spot his or her productivity patterns for a day with the help of vivid color palette.

Starting the task sets user's mind in a working mood. Stopping the task switches the type of activity. Intuitive controls make tasks management fast and easy.

Summarized insights covering user's performance and time spent on tasks or projects by days, weeks or months.

Personalized working environment to user's tastes of productive and productive applications and websites.

Option to block distracting websites with built-in automatic website blocker on Safari, Firefox or Chrome.

To make Y-Productive brand closer to our users, a brand's personality was developed. Bee serves as a representative of the brand. It fascilitates dedication, efficiency, concentration, and expertise.

For all our digital materials, from simple emails to informal communications with the user, we have simplified our Mr Bee's form to make it closer to the user. Little experiments gave us a range of possibilities we might also go into. Choose the one you like :)
